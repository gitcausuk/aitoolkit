Assessment title: Strategic recommendation for a live organisational challenge

Students submit a 2,500-word individual report analysing a case organisation and making a recommendation to its senior leadership. The report should use course concepts and relevant evidence. It is worth 40% of the course mark.

Learning outcomes assessed:
1. Apply relevant theoretical frameworks to a complex organisational problem.
2. Evaluate incomplete and conflicting evidence.
3. Reach and communicate a defensible strategic recommendation.
4. Consider implementation risks and ethical implications.

Current marking criteria:
- Understanding of course concepts: 25%
- Quality of analysis: 35%
- Recommendation: 25%
- Structure and presentation: 15%
