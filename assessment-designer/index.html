Design for credible evidence of learning

Redesign an assessment for the age of AI

Upload or paste a brief and produce a redesign with clear AI permissions, marking criteria and an implementation checklist.

● Files are processed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded by this app.

● The tool itself contains no AI. It uses fixed, transparent rules to produce a draft for review; the optional AI prompt step is separate and labelled.

How suggestions are produced

The Designer matches entered information to predefined disciplinary, format, permission and evidence rules. It does not predict quality, authorship or approval. Qualitative indicators and suggested weights are editable starting points, not validated measurements.
