# 1.3.0 - 24 July 2026

- Updated the public Oxford policy links and source-check date.
- Clarified that Toolkit permission levels are drafting aids rather than an approved University taxonomy.
- Reframed permission suggestions as draft outputs for local review.
- Upgraded PDF.js and Mammoth dependencies, added a content-security policy and disabled PDF dynamic evaluation.
- Added accessible tab semantics and keyboard navigation to the generated report.
- Strengthened privacy wording around document parsing and third-party dependencies.
- Softened claims that could imply validated risk measurement, proof of authorship or central University endorsement.

# 1.2.0

- Removed pseudo-precise numerical VIVA readiness scores.
- Added transparent qualitative review indicators and explanations.
- Added “Why this result?” for permission suggestions.
- Clarified that disciplinary matching, exposure labels, route ordering and marking weights are deterministic heuristics or editable defaults.
- Added visible methodology guidance while preserving the existing design.

# Changelog

## 1.1.0 - 20 July 2026

- Editorial pass to reduce word count: shorter status strip, hero lead, footer and source note; caveats consolidated rather than repeated.
